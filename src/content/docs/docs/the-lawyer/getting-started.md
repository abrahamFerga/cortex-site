Your workspace is created the moment your subscription completes — the sign-in link arrives in
the admin's inbox. No setup call, no waiting.

## First steps

1. **Sign in** with the link from your welcome email. You land in the chat — the assistant is
   matter-centric, so most things start with a matter.
2. **Open your first matter**: type *"Onboard a new client: conflict check, open the matter, and
   prepare the engagement letter"*. The assistant runs the conflict check first, reports the
   result, and only then opens the matter.
3. **Docket something**: *"The answer is due August 14"* — it offers to docket the deadline with
   reminders.
4. **Invite your team** (Team plan): Admin → Users. Seats come from your subscription; you can
   change them any time in the customer portal.

## Where things live

| Surface | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Chat** | The assistant — every action permission-checked and audited |
| **Matters** | Every engagement workspace: parties, deadlines, tasks, time, documents |
| **Deadlines / Tasks** | Cross-matter docket and to-do views |
| **Admin** | Users and roles, AI settings, audit log, usage |

## Approvals

Any action that changes the record — filing a document, closing a matter — waits for a human
approval in the chat's approvals panel. That's the design: the assistant does the work, you
keep signature authority.
